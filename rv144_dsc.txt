### Description ###
The RV144 dataset contains 246 observations (vaccine recipients), outcome variable (y), two clinical covariates (gender and behavioral_risk), and a total of 194 biomarkers.

### Major variables ###
y - outcome variable (0 or 1)
gender - clinical covariate (M or F)
behavioral_risk - clinical covariate (Low, Medium, High)
IgG - IgG antibodies
IgA - IgA antibodies
IgAC1 - binding of IgA antibodies to the C1 region of envelope protein

In the anonymized RV144 dataset ("rv144.csv" file), there are 194 biomarkers including IgG, IgA, IgAC1, and the other 191 biomarkers.
